Downloadable Content List Component Block : Use in Brand Detail Copy Block
=======================================================================


Modify text content as you wish in sample.html, then copy paste it in wysisyg editor.

After have uploaded your pdf in Media section of EPIServer, for each item select the text "link to your pdf" in wysisyg editor then click on "insert/Edit link", select "Media" then look for your pdf file then click OK. The text "link to your pdf" will disappear and the link containing your pdf will wrap all the block.
